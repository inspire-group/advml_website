Adversarial ML @ Princeton's INSPIRE Group

The INSPIRE Group at Princeton University's Electrical Engineering department conducts research on a broad variety of topics in privacy and security. This website documents our research on adversarial machine learning. We work on all aspects of this problem, including developing new attacks, designing defenses and exploring the underlying fundamental theory.
